Our Menu

Chicken

A garlic mayonnaise goes perfectly with these
tender grilled chicken thighs, flavored
with rosemary and served with new potatoes
mashed with a fork.

Beef

A generous galette of a mixture of AAA beef and
house spices all garnished and enhanced with
our original rib sauce accompanied
by our house coleslaw.

Sushi

It is a sushi in the shape of a roll. The rice is
put on the seaweed sheet (nori) and rolled.
Inside there are ingredients like kampyo
(a kind of dried vegetable), cucumber etc.
To have the shape of a roll, there is
a bamboo "makisu" tool.
